Electric Vehicles Statistics in Washington State

This analysis focuses on Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV's) registered in Washington State as of the end of 2020.  The analysis presents descriptive statistics as well as correlations between vehicle popularity as measured in the number of registered cars and several factors including:

  • The driving range of the vehicle
  • The MSRP of the vehicle
  • The make and model of the vehicle

Correlations are also included between the density of cars within a specific county and factors including:

  • The population of the county of residence
  • The cost of electricity in the county
  • The number of charging stations in the county
